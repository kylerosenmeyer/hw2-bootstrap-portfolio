A Little Bit About Me

Scroll Down to Read More.

Kyle Rosenmeyer has been an active photographer for 2 years, focused on creating artisic landscapes and cityscapes. Kyle has traveled thousands of miles by car, boat, plane, train, and on foot with a camera, from central Alberta to central Florida. Kyle received a formal education from Boise State University in Civil Engineering, and has gone on to work on infrastructure projects in Idaho and Florida, ranging from transportation to land development and municpal planning.

He has received online certificates in business and entrepreneurship from Harvard University and is currently pursuing a certificate in web development from the University of Central Florida. This website showcases the portfolio work from Kyle's web development and photography efforts.
